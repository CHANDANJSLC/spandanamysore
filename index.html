This vital initiative was born out of the needs of the transgender community. Our main objective is to facilitate the integration of TG individuals into mainstream society, supporting individuals at all stages of their journey. We offer services including secure housing, educational scholarships, nutritious meals, and advocate for awareness and acceptance of the transgender community. We welcome everyone to join us in creating a compassionate and inclusive society by seeking support and participating in our mission.

• Aid and Assistance

If you or someone you know within the TG Community is facing challenges with housing, education, particularly those under the age of 18, but not limited to this age group, we're here to support you. Whether you're looking to improve your life through education or seeking stable accommodations while pursuing career opportunities, please reach out to us. Spandana is dedicated to providing assistance and resources to transgender individuals striving for a better future. Contact us, and let us help you on your journey.
